A Wufoo Form Manager for WordPress

== Description ==

= It's So Easy ... =

WuPhooey lets you see your entries and add a form to your blog all inside of WordPress. Simply click on the WuPhooey icon while editing a post and choose the form you want in the dropdown. Done.

= Features =

* Lightweight
* Easy to add forms to posts
* See your entries inside of WordPress

== Installation ==

1. Unzip WuPhooey.zip into the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Enter your information on the settings page in `WuPhooey > Settings`
1. Create a new page in WordPress and choose your form from the WuPhooey button dropdown
